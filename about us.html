About Us





Prince Milk Tea and Cafe founded on the love for Milk Tea and Coffee and it is fueled by the passion for quality drinks and foods. All our drinks are exclusively formulated to leave a distict mark of quality in the minds of our customers.


Founded in 2020, Prince Milk Tea and Cafe is a local café located in Quezon City, Philippines. This dream 2-storey shop stemmed from the passion of two-inspiring and creative Filipino entrepreneurs who have a BIG love for good food.


At Prince Milk Tea and Cafe, we aspire to be one of Philippines’ top leading coffee and tea shophouse with the primary focus of providing our customers with affordable, freshly brewed hot and cold beverages (with a unique twist), and quality-flavourful soul food in a cozy environment.
